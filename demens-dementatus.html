Overview

Demens Dementatus is a game made for the GOTO gameJam. On this edition of the jam the keyword was "states". With this, we had in mind two main focus, one is the development of a polished, inmersive, oneiric atmosphere that can resemble, at some point, the view of a person with senile dementia. And two, to have a full playeable game.

I created all the art, assets and UI for the game.

I contribute to the design and implementacion of the game mechanics.

I contribute to the narrative.

Character design

We try to use a single template and derive the designs from there. On the one hand is the playable character who will have two states: young and old. On the other hand, there is the "spectre" that will make appearances at different times and finally the son, who reveals his identity at the end of the game.

Map/atmosphere

For the size of the map we wanted to shorten the scope to something we could develop and modify without having to make a lot of changes. It occurred to us to work with a simple room with a closet and a window towards "nothing", this would give a feeling of isolation and alienation.
For the atmosphere of the game, the combination of music with the main effect of the shaders was very important to us, this played a very important role in the construction of the world.

Game mechanichs

The main mechanism of Demens is to switch between the two "realities" finding objects and clues to the code that will help open his safe and become lucid again. These clues are scattered throughout the room.
