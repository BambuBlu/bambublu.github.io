## Introduction (June 12, 2025)

Welcome to the first post in this series documenting the development of a secure Android password manager app. In this space, I will share the entire process — from initial technical decisions to implementation and testing.

The app aims to provide a reliable and practical solution for users to securely store their passwords, leveraging best practices in data encryption, local storage architecture, and user authentication.

Beyond technical aspects, I will also cover UI and UX design considerations to ensure the app is not only secure but also intuitive and easy to use.

Each update will reflect a step in the iterative development and testing cycle, so anyone interested can follow along and learn alongside this project.
